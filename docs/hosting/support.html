How to Use the App

Send files directly between nearby devices on the same Wi‑Fi network — no account required.

  1. Connect both devices to the same Wi‑Fi network.
  2. On the receiving device, open the Receive tab and tap Start Receiving. A QR code and 4-digit PIN will appear.
  3. On the sending device, open the Send tab, tap Scan Receiver QR, scan the code, and enter the PIN when prompted.
  4. Tap Select Files, choose your photos, videos, or documents, then tap Send Directly.
  5. Transfer progress appears on both devices. Received files show in the Received Files list on the receiver.
Tip: You can also pick a device from Nearby Receivers on the Send screen when both devices are on the same Wi‑Fi.

Common Questions

Transfer fails or devices are not found Make sure both devices are on the same Wi‑Fi network. Guest or corporate networks often block device-to-device traffic. Keep the receiver app open with receiving active during the entire transfer.
Camera permission required Camera access is only needed when you tap Scan Receiver QR on the Send screen. You can allow it when iOS prompts you.
Local Network permission (iOS) iOS may ask for Local Network access so the app can discover nearby receivers and transfer files over Wi‑Fi. Please tap Allow when prompted.
Large file transfers The app supports chunked uploads for large files. Keep both devices awake and connected to Wi‑Fi until the transfer completes.
Can I send files without Wi‑Fi? On supported Apple devices, offline Multipeer transfer may work between two iPhones using Bluetooth when Wi‑Fi is unavailable. Start receiving on one device and use Nearby Receivers on the other.
Why do I see ads? File Share is free and may show Google AdMob ads (app open and banner ads). Ads do not block file transfer and do not receive your files or transfer data.
Export received files After receiving files, you can open, save, share, or delete them from the Received Files list. Sharing via AirDrop or other apps is optional and handled by iOS.

Requirements

  • Both devices usually need to be on the same Wi‑Fi network
  • The receiver should keep the app open and receiving during transfer
  • No account or sign-in is required
  • Files are transferred device-to-device — not uploaded to our servers
